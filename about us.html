About Us

Poorna Kamala Vidhyala , school established in the year of 2007. We, the PKV Family encourage the hidden talents and help to enlight the student's future. Its been 16 year's , and we have best experienced teacher's. Till now we had the best journey with our students and staff.

Poorna Kamala Vidhyalaya aims towards institutional excellence through the character which includes the equality of teaching, the infrastructure that it provides, through its curricular and Cocurricular activities and thus aims towards the integrated development of the mind, body and heart of the pupils.

The program will promote an appreciation of aesthetics, a love for learning and knowledge and nurturing their potential to become persons who have the capacity for thinking, problem solving, creativity and ability and ability to undertake self directed learning.

We aim to cultivate a balanced development of knowledge,humanitarian values, inculcate patriotism, to imibe honesty, educational, courage good conduct and behaviour of a universal outlook with a commitment to social justice and equity.

The program will enable the pupils to develop as persons who are committed to innovation, quality and excellence in any task they undertake.
